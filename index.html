About

Professional Summary

RL Engineer and robotics researcher with about 4 years of experience in reinforcement learning, model predictive control, and sim-to-real deployment for legged and humanoid robots. Experienced across the full robotics development cycle: simulation, policy training, C++/ROS 2 deployment, hardware validation, and publication-quality research.

Experience

June 2024 - Present

RL Engineer, MIPT

Laboratory of Wave Processes and Controlling Systems

  • Developed and trained RL policies for humanoid robots, including walking, soccer skills, balance recovery, and trajectory-following dance motions.
  • Built and tuned simulations in NVIDIA Isaac Sim, Isaac Gym, MuJoCo, and PyBullet, including dynamics, contacts, and sensor configuration.
  • Deployed trained policies to real robots using high-performance C++ and ROS/ROS 2 hardware integration pipelines.
  • Reduced the sim-to-real gap using domain randomization, validation stands, sensor calibration, and noise filtering.
  • Developed an MJX differentiable-simulation method for robot parameter identification from motion tracks, without torque sensors.
  • Achieved a 75% reduction in turning deviation, a 46% increase in traveled distance, and 1.88x better trajectory tracking accuracy on custom hardware.
